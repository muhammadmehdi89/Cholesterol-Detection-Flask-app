About

The objective of our project is to develop and evaluate a multi-class deep learning model for accurate classification of lipid profile test results based on serum cholesterol, triglycerides, HDL, and LDL levels, aiming to provide users with reliable and actionable health insights through automated analysis of their test reports. The project combines hardware, including a webcam on a tripod stand, with a software stack comprising Flask for backend development and HTML for frontend interface. A deep learning model processes images of lipid profile test reports captured by the webcam, classifying them into predefined categories.
